Software Engineer — Backend & APIs

About the role
We are looking for a mid-level software engineer to design, build, and operate
backend services. You will work with TypeScript and Node.js, collaborate with
product and frontend teams, and help improve reliability and performance.

Requirements
- 3+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong TypeScript and Node.js
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
- Familiarity with REST API design and testing

Nice to have
- React experience for occasional full-stack work
- Experience with observability tools (logging, metrics, tracing)

Responsibilities
- Implement and maintain backend services and integrations
- Participate in code reviews and on-call rotations
- Partner with stakeholders to clarify requirements and deliver incrementally
